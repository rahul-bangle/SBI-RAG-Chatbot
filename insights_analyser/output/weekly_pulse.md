# Weekly Pulse: Product Insights

## Key Themes
- **UI/UX Satisfaction:** High praise for the intuitive, clean design and user-friendly interface.
- **Performance Concerns:** Reports of lag during peak market hours and occasional login issues.
- **Support Gaps:** Significant frustration regarding slow query resolution and support team responsiveness.
- **Value Proposition:** Users appreciate low brokerage and comprehensive features for long-term investing.

## User Perspectives
> "Very easy to use and intuitive interface for trading."
> "App keeps lagging during market hours. Need performance improvements."
> "My query is not resolved for a week. Support is pathetic."

## Action Items
1. **Performance Optimization:** Prioritize engineering resources to address latency and stability issues during peak market hours.
2. **Support Workflow Audit:** Review and improve the ticket escalation process to reduce query resolution time.
3. **UX Refinement:** Investigate menu navigation to surface hidden, high-value features.

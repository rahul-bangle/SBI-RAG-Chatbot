Subject: Weekly Pulse: User Insights to Inform Our Product Roadmap

Dear Product and Growth Teams,

This week's pulse note highlights key user insights that can inform our product development and growth strategies. The top themes that emerged from user feedback are:

* Ease of use: Users appreciate our app's clean interface and ease of tracking mutual funds.
* Technical issues: Users are experiencing problems with transactions, login, and KYC verification.
* Support and transparency: Users are frustrated with unresponsive support and lack of clarity on issues.

Some real user quotes that illustrate these themes include:
* "Very easy to use and track my mutual funds. The interface is clean."
* "My KYC has been pending for 2 weeks. Very frustrating. No support response."
* "The app keeps asking for OTP again and again. Unable to login since morning."

To address these concerns, we propose the following action ideas:
* Fix UPI gateway issues to prevent transaction failures.
* Improve support response time and provide clear updates on KYC and redemption status.
* Optimize app loading time to enhance user experience.

Let's work together to prioritize these initiatives and enhance our user experience.
